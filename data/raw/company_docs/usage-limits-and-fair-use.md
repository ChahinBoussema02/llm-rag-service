# Usage Limits & Fair Use (AcmeAI)

## Usage Limits
- Free: 20 requests/day
- Pro: 2,000 requests/month
- Team: 12,000 requests/month shared across seats

## Fair Use
Abuse includes:
- Automated scraping at high frequency
- Attempts to bypass limits
- Sharing Team seats outside the organization

## Enforcement
- First violation: warning email and temporary rate limit (1 request/10 seconds) for 24 hours.
- Second violation: account suspended for 7 days.
- Third violation: account termination.

## Effect on Refunds
- Accounts flagged for abuse are not eligible for refunds.
