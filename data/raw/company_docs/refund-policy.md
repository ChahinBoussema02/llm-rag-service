# Refund Policy (AcmeAI)

## Eligibility
- Pro and Team plans are eligible for refunds within 14 days of the initial purchase.
- Refunds apply only to first-time subscriptions (first purchase on an account).

## Non-Refundable
- Free plan is non-refundable.
- Add-on seats purchased mid-cycle are non-refundable.
- Usage-based overages (if enabled) are non-refundable.

## Exceptions
- If the service is unavailable due to a confirmed incident lasting more than 24 hours, partial refunds may be issued at AcmeAI's discretion.
- Refund requests may be denied if there is evidence of abuse (see Fair Use policy).

## How to Request
- Contact support with the subject "Refund Request" and include the account email and purchase date.
