# Support Policy (AcmeAI)

## Channels
- Free: community forum only
- Pro: email support (response target: 48 business hours)
- Team: priority email support (response target: 24 business hours)

## Scope
Support covers:
- billing issues, login/access, product bugs, and usage questions

Support does not cover:
- custom model training
- writing prompts for you
- legal advice

## Refund Requests
Refund requests must follow the Refund Policy and may be denied for Fair Use violations.
