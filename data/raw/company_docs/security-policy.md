# Security Policy (AcmeAI)

## Access Controls
- Team plan supports SSO.
- Admins must enable MFA for all Team admins.
- Passwords must be at least 12 characters.

## Data Handling
- Customer documents uploaded to the service are encrypted at rest.
- Access to production data is restricted to on-call engineers and security team members.

## Vulnerability Reporting
- Report security issues to security@acmeai.example (do not post publicly).
- We aim to respond within 72 hours.

## Policy Violations
- Intentional misuse of access privileges may result in termination of access and account cancellation.
