# Billing & Plans (AcmeAI)

## Plans
### Free
- Price: €0/month
- Usage: up to 20 requests/day
- Features: basic chat, community support
- Data retention: 7 days

### Pro
- Price: €29/month
- Usage: up to 2,000 requests/month
- Features: tool access, file upload up to 20MB, email support
- Data retention: 30 days

### Team
- Price: €99/month (up to 5 seats)
- Usage: up to 12,000 requests/month (shared)
- Features: SSO, admin dashboard, priority email support
- Data retention: 90 days

## Billing Cycle
- Subscriptions renew monthly on the purchase date.
- Usage resets at the start of each billing cycle.

## Payment Failures
- If payment fails, the account enters "past due" immediately.
- After 3 days past due, access is limited to Free plan features.
- After 14 days past due, the subscription is canceled.

## Plan Changes
- Upgrades take effect immediately; the plan price is prorated for the remaining billing cycle.
- Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle renewal date.
